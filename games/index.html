Infamous Second Son review

by: cade guevara

plot

infamous second son is the third game to the infamous series. infamous second son is a third person open world action-adventure game that anyone can enjoy.

you play as a young protagonist that is named Delsin Rowe. delsin is an american indian who is apart of the akomish reservation. delsin didnt always have powers, but when a truck filled with a few conduits crashed on his tribe's reservation, he gained the powers of one of the conduits. what is a conduit you might ask. a conduit is a human being who can channel superhuman powers and use them to their advantage. a lady from D.U.P (the Department of Unified Protection. which is a group that is against conduits a.k.a bio-terrorist) then found out that bio-terrorists have esscaped and went to the reservation where the crash happened. the conduits were long gone and the only person that knew about their where abouts was Delsin. the woman the interrogated Delsin and the whole tribe as well. none of them spoke, so she put concrete spikes through each and every one of them as a punishment. she did that with her superhuman powers, because she is also a conduit. this cold-hearted woman is known as augustine. the only way the tribe can heal back to normal is by the way they got it... with the power. obviously augustine wont do this so delsin needs to journey on to seattle and absorb augustines power. with her power, he can heal the whole tribe.
